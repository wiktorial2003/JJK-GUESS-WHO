JJK Guess Who Online

Host on GitHub Pages and play with a friend using Firebase Realtime Database.

Room Setup

Player 1 creates a room. Player 2 joins with the same room code or invite link.

How to Play

  1. Create or join a room.
  2. Both players pick a secret JJK character.
  3. Take turns asking yes/no questions.
  4. Click cards on your board to eliminate characters.
  5. Guess the opponent's character to win.

The answers are verified automatically from the opponent's selected character.
